Układ mechaniczny - model 36800

The Europa lever is a unique mechanical brake and mechanical accelerator column mounted on the right or left of the steering wheel. No more tidious push and pull mechanism that hits your leg, or rods impeding your ingres or egress from the vehicle. The Europa lever accelerator is operated by a smooth radial action with the lever in a fixed position, and braking is affected by simply pushing the same lever forward which will disingage the acceleration immediately. The Europa lever simply progresssive action, allows the oppurtunity for an easy and safe driving. KEY FEATURES • Forward movement of the lever to brake. • Does not alter any original part of the vehicle. • No intrusion of ingress or egress from the vehicle. • Non encumbrance under the steering wheel space. • The lever does not touch the driver's leg. • Minimal force to operate the accelerator. • When braking the accelerator disengages
