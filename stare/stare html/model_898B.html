Panel - model 898B

Fixed Secondary controls operated from a static control stick behind the steering wheel. The D898B offers another flexible way of operating the Horn, Indicators and Headlights. The device is adaptable to almost every type of vehicle. It comes in a number of high-quality finishes and can be positioned to suit individual needs. Key Features Secondary controls at your fingertips Interacts with original controls Several high quality finishes available Available with CanBus technology
