Układ kierowniczy - Model 906GVQR

The 906V device is an "electronic Accelerator Ring" mounted on top of the steering wheel, now available in a Quick-Release version. One smooth operation "simply pressing a button on the bracket" consents the rapid removal of the Accelerator Ring. As we strive to guarantee the highest possible level of safety for our clients and we have inhibited acceleration unless the device is correctly mounted.
