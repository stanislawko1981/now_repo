Mechaniczny układ hamulcowy - Model 907P

The brake lever is anchored to a clamp and then fixed to the original anchor point of the driver’s seat. This placement totally eliminates clutter under the steering wheel. The leather covered brake knob has two function buttons. One button activates the horn and one initiates a brake lock to facilitate starting on an incline or hill. The unobtrusive design of this device allows the driver full discretion regarding steering wheel height and depth settings.
